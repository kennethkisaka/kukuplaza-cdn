STUFFED CHICKEN BREAST WITH CHICKEN STEW

Stuffed Chicken Breast with Chicken Stew is a hearty, flavorful dish. The chicken breast is filled with a delicious stuffing, then baked or pan-seared to perfection, served with a rich chicken stew on the side.

Ingredients for Stuffed Chicken Breast:

  • 4 boneless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup spinach (blanched and chopped)
  • 1/4 cup cream cheese or ricotta
  • 1/4 cup shredded mozzarella or cheddar cheese
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Toothpicks for securing the stuffed chicken

Ingredients for Chicken Stew:

  • 500g chicken thighs or drumsticks
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 carrots (peeled and sliced)
  • 2 potatoes (peeled and diced)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Stuffing: In a bowl, mix together the chopped spinach, cream cheese, shredded cheese, and minced garlic. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Stuff the Chicken Breasts: Slice the chicken breasts horizontally to create a pocket. Be careful not to cut all the way through. Fill each pocket with the spinach-cheese mixture and secure with toothpicks. Season the stuffed chicken breasts with paprika, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  3. Cook the Chicken Breasts: In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Sear the stuffed chicken breasts for 3-4 minutes per side, until golden brown. Transfer to a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F) for 15-20 minutes to cook through. If you don’t want to bake, continue cooking in the pan on lower heat until the chicken is fully cooked.
  4. Prepare the Chicken Stew: In a separate large pot, heat vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, sauté until softened. Add cumin, turmeric, and paprika, and stir for a minute. Add the chopped tomatoes and tomato paste. Cook until the tomatoes soften and the oil starts to separate.
  5. Add Vegetables and Chicken: Add the carrots, potatoes, chicken thighs or drumsticks, and chicken broth to the pot. Season with salt and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 30-35 minutes, until the vegetables are tender and the chicken is fully cooked.
  6. Serve: Once the stuffed chicken breasts are ready, serve them alongside the hearty chicken stew. Garnish with fresh parsley or cilantro.

Enjoy your delicious Stuffed Chicken Breast with Chicken Stew!
