CHICKEN SHAWARMA

Chicken Shawarma is a Middle Eastern dish where marinated chicken is roasted and wrapped in pita bread. Itâ€™s packed with flavors from a blend of spices, garlic, and lemon, often served with garlic sauce and fresh veggies.

For Chicken Marinade:

  • 500g boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts
  • 3 tablespoons plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Garlic Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt to taste

For Serving:

  • Pita bread or flatbreads
  • Chopped lettuce
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Thinly sliced onions
  • Cucumber slices
  • Pickles (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Chicken: In a large bowl, mix yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, and all the spices (cumin, coriander, paprika, turmeric, cinnamon, allspice, salt, and pepper). Add the chicken thighs or breasts and coat them well with the marinade. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or overnight for better flavor.
  2. Cook the Chicken: Preheat your grill or skillet over medium heat. Cook the marinated chicken on the grill or pan, about 5-6 minutes per side, until fully cooked and charred slightly on the edges. Let the chicken rest for a few minutes, then slice thinly.
  3. Prepare the Garlic Sauce: In a small bowl, mix together the mayonnaise, yogurt, minced garlic, lemon juice, and a pinch of salt. Stir until well combined and creamy.
  4. Assemble the Shawarma: Warm the pita bread on the grill or in a pan. Spread a layer of garlic sauce on the bread. Add the sliced chicken, lettuce, tomatoes, onions, cucumbers, and pickles (if using). Drizzle more garlic sauce on top.
  5. Serve: Roll up the pita around the filling or fold it into a wrap. Serve the Chicken Shawarma with extra garlic sauce or a side of fries.

Enjoy your delicious homemade Chicken Shawarma!
