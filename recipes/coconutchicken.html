COCONUT CHICKEN

Coconut Chicken is a rich, creamy dish with tender chicken cooked in a coconut-based sauce. It's flavored with aromatic spices, herbs, and the creamy goodness of coconut milk, making it a deliciously comforting meal.

For Chicken Marinade:

  • 500g chicken (with bones or boneless)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons oil

For Coconut Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2-3 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro (coriander leaves)
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves (optional)

For Garnishing:

  • Fresh cilantro leaves
  • 2 tablespoons roasted cashew nuts (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Chicken: In a large bowl, mix the ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chili powder, ground cumin, lemon juice, oil, and salt. Add the chicken pieces and marinate for at least 1 hour (or overnight for best results).
  2. Cook the Chicken: Heat oil or ghee in a large pan. Add the marinated chicken and sear on medium heat until golden brown. Remove the chicken from the pan and set it aside.
  3. Prepare the Coconut Sauce: In the same pan, heat some more oil if needed and add cumin seeds. Once they start to crackle, add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft and the oil separates.
  4. Add Spices: Add slit green chilies, ground coriander, and garam masala to the pan. Stir well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Combine with Chicken: Return the seared chicken to the pan. Pour in the coconut milk and stir well to combine. Let it simmer for 15-20 minutes until the chicken is fully cooked and the sauce thickens. Add chopped cilantro and mint (if using) at the end.
  6. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves, roasted cashew nuts, and a squeeze of lemon juice. Serve the coconut chicken with rice or flatbread.

Enjoy your delicious homemade Coconut Chicken!
