LEMON HERB ROASTED CHICKEN

Lemon Herb Roasted Chicken is a classic dish, full of bright flavors from lemon and fresh herbs. It’s juicy on the inside with a crispy, golden skin, perfect for a family meal or special occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken (about 1.5kg)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 large lemon, zested and quartered
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 cup chicken broth or water
  • Extra fresh herbs and lemon slices for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 190°C (375°F). Place a rack in the center of the oven.
  2. Prepare the Chicken: Pat the chicken dry with paper towels. Rub olive oil all over the chicken, including the cavity. Season generously with salt, black pepper, and paprika.
  3. Stuff the Chicken: Stuff the cavity with the quartered lemon and a few sprigs of fresh herbs (rosemary, thyme, parsley).
  4. Make the Herb Mixture: In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, lemon zest, chopped rosemary, thyme, and parsley. Rub this herb mixture all over the chicken, especially under the skin for extra flavor.
  5. Roast the Chicken: Place the chicken, breast-side up, in a roasting pan. Pour 1 cup of chicken broth or water into the pan to keep the chicken moist. Roast for about 1 hour 20 minutes, or until the internal temperature of the chicken reaches 75°C (165°F) and the skin is golden and crispy. Baste the chicken with the pan juices every 20-30 minutes.
  6. Rest the Chicken: Once the chicken is fully cooked, remove it from the oven and let it rest for 10-15 minutes before carving. This helps the juices redistribute, making the meat more tender.
  7. Garnish and Serve: Garnish the roasted chicken with extra fresh herbs and lemon slices if desired. Serve with roasted vegetables, potatoes, or a side salad.

Enjoy your flavorful and juicy Lemon Herb Roasted Chicken!
